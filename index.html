Kaiden Jones

British independent researcher and author. I formalise clear, testable models in logic and systems theory, and write fiction exploring science and memory. Projects include Half-Silvering Dimensions and the novel 2938: Atlantis.

See selected work

About

I prefer accessible, well-structured writing over jargon. My work focuses on building rigorous logical scaffolding first, then exploring applications across disciplines. I collaborate with education and community partners across the North West to support care-experienced young people and to share research in plain English.
